Why Multi-Channel Fulfillment?

Amazon Multi-Channel Fulfillment (MCF) leverages the world’s largest fulfillment network with more than 200 fulfillment centers globally to fulfill ecommerce orders wherever they’re placed.

On-time delivery

Deliver your products as quickly as 1, 2, or 3-5 business days after their ship date with a >97% on-time delivery rate. Deliveries are made 7 days a week.

Low fulfillment costs

Up to 60% discounts on multi-unit orders. One fee for pick, pack, and ship. Pay only for fulfillment and storage.

100+ integrations*

Connect to your channels with pre-built applications, like BigCommerce and Shopify, or directly through our APIs for order automation.
*includes prebuilt and developer-friendly integrations

Streamlined workflow

Pooled inventory saves you time and money and Amazon's fulfillment network will automatically select carriers to meet your customers promises.

Future-proof operations

Amazon has invested $30 billion since 2019 to fuel ecommerce seller success.

Fully trackable orders

Receive tracking numbers within 24 hours of order placement for priority and expedited shipments placed against on-hand inventory to comply with external marketplace guidelines.
